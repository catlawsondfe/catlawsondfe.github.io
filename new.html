Good estate management for schools (GEMS) - what's new

A summary of changes and updates to the previous version of GEMS.

We first published GEMS in April 2018 to help schools and responsible bodies manage their land and buildings. It was very well received, so we havenâ€™t made a large amount of changes.

While some of the changes are updates due to new guidance or legislation, most are in response to feedback from users in schools and responsible bodies.

New tools and checklists

The list of tool and checklists, includes:

  • a list of the benefits of good estate management
  • an interactive version of the GEMS self-assessment tool which will automatically generate an action plan for you
  • an interactive tool to help you track compliance with health and safety and building regulations and inspections
  • updates to the top 10 questions for governing boards
  • a new tool to help you prioritise maintenance projects and tasks
  • a checklist that you can use to reduce energy and water usage

Changes to navigation

We have changed some of the headings and the order in which they are presented in response to your feedback.

We have also combined the sections on managing the performance of your buildings with sustainability, to make it easier to make sure your premises are operating efficiently and effectively.

You should also find it easier to move through GEMS and find what youâ€™re looking for.

New guidance and information

We have reviewed all the links and signposts to further information, so they are all up to date at the time of publication.

Fundamentals of good estate management

The fundamentals of good estate management section helps you see at a glance the key requirements. Itâ€™s been reviewed and refreshed to reflect changes to the rest of the guidance.

Health and safety

We have updated the health and safety section. It includes more information on responsibilities, and updated guidance and links from the Health and Safety Executive. You can use this section with our new compliance tracker to help you keep on top of your responsibilities.

Skills and knowledge

We have updated the skills and knowledge section, which now provides links to:

  • the competency framework for governing boards
  • ISBL professional standards
  • a new competency framework under development for school estate managers
  • how to secure professional support

Buying goods and services

We have also updated the buying goods and services section to make it easier to read. We have updated the links to other sources of support, including the recommended framework to help you buy and manage specialist professional services. We also list the latest deals available to schools.

Understanding tenure and land ownership

We have also clarified some of the content in the understanding tenure and land ownership section.
